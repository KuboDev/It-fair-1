About Mount Everest

Mount Everest is Earth's highest mountain, located in the Himalayas on the border of Nepal and the Tibet Autonomous Region of China. Its peak is 8,848.86 meters (29,031.7 feet) above sea level.

Everest attracts many climbers, including highly experienced mountaineers and capable climbers willing to hire professional guides. It offers breathtaking views, adventure, and a challenge for those brave enough to take it on.

Visitor Information

  • Best Time to Visit: April to May and September to November.
  • Weather: Cold, with temperatures dropping to -60°C (-76°F) near the summit.
  • Travel Tips: Acclimatize to the altitude, bring warm clothing, and ensure proper gear for hiking.
